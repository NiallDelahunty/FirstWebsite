Welcome! I am very passionate about STEM and one of my favourite parts of being in the world of science is being able to help others discover how interesting these topics can be (or to pass a test). My background is in biomedical sciences, I have a research masters in molecular biology but have recently made the move into the world of computers. So of course my favourite subject to teach is Biology, but that said I am equally delighted to help with maths, chemistry, JC science or python programming. I have given grinds all through college and worked as a teaching assistant in the school of medicine in UCD. I teach biology anywhere up to and inlcuding university level in biology, and to junior or leaving certificate in all other topics.
